Petra

Inhebited since prehistoric times, this Nabatean caravan-city. situated between the Red Sea and the Dead Sea, was an important crossroads between Arabia. Egypt and Syria-Phoenicia. Petra is half-built, half-carved into the rock, and is surrounded by mountains riddled with passages and gorges. It is one of the world's most famous archaeological sites, where ancient Eastern tradtions blend with Hellenistic architecture.
